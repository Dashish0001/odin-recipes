Nishi

Description

This is a recipe for Nishi, a traditional dish from Japan.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice in a colander until the water runs clear.
  2. Add the rice, water, and salt to a pot.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
