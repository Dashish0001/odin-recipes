Pani puri

Description

This is a recipe for Pani Puri, a traditional dish from India.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup meda
  • 2 cups water
  • imli powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • dry grams sabji
  • oil
  • chat masala

Steps

  1. make the meda mixture with water, salt, and imli powder.
  2. fry the meda in oil until golden brown.
  3. Prepare the dry grams sabji.
  4. Dip the fried meda fulki in the syrup and serve.
